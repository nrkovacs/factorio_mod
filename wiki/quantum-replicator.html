Quantum replicator

Quantum replicator
TypeCrafting machine
Prototypequantum-replicator
Unlocked byQuantum replication
Stats15 MW operating drain; crafting speed 2; 4 module slots

The Quantum replicator converts interstellar dust into strategic raw materials and exotic late-game products.

Obtaining

  • Crafted after researching Quantum replication.

Usage

  • Crafts all recipes in the interstellar replication category.
  • Provides closed-loop production for missing platform resources.

Recipe

AmountIngredient
1Electromagnetic plant
100Quantum processor
200Superconductor

Mechanics

The replicator is derived from electromagnetic plant technology. It uses high power and module slots to make dust conversion scalable, but recipe costs keep it from replacing planet-scale mining too early.
