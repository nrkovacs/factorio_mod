Antimatter

Antimatter
TypeFuel component
Prototypeantimatter
Unlocked byQuantum replication
Stats120 seconds in a Quantum replicator

Antimatter is an ultra-expensive drive fuel created from interstellar dust and fusion power cells.

Obtaining

  • Produced in the Quantum replicator using the Replicate antimatter recipe.

Usage

  • Consumed by Antimatter drives during fleet boost calculations.

Recipe

AmountIngredient
2000Interstellar dust
10Fusion power cell

Mechanics

Antimatter converts collected interstellar matter into a scarce, high-leverage speed resource. Because antimatter drives provide four drive-power units each, antimatter demand scales with both drive count and relativistic speed.
