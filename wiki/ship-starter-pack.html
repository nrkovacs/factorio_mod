Ship starter pack

Ship starter pack
TypeUtility item
Prototypeship-starter-pack
Unlocked byFleet printing

Ship starter packs package enough critical infrastructure to bootstrap or merge an interstellar fleet.

Obtaining

  • Crafted after researching Fleet printing.

Usage

  • Used by the fleet control systems to create or expand interstellar fleets.
  • Represents foundation, drives, labs, and replicators compacted into a fleet seed.

Recipe

AmountIngredient
1000Space platform foundation
4Stellar fusion drive
4Interstellar lab

Mechanics

This item avoids manually shipping every fleet bootstrap component separately. Its high cost keeps fleet creation tied to late Space Age logistics.
