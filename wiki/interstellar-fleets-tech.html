Interstellar fleets

Interstellar fleets
Prototypeinterstellar-fleets
PrerequisitesPromethium science pack
Research cost2000 cycles, 60 seconds each, using automation, logistic, chemical, production, utility, space, metallurgic, electromagnetic, agricultural, cryogenic, and promethium science packs.

Interstellar fleets unlocks the first layer of deep-space fleet infrastructure and travel to the galactic center.

Unlocks

  • Interstellar lab
  • Interstellar dust collector
  • Stellar fusion drive
  • Galactic center space location

Strategy

Research this when promethium science is stable and you can afford high-end platform components. It establishes the dust economy and fleet propulsion base used by the rest of the mod.
