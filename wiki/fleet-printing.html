Fleet printing

Fleet printing
Prototypefleet-printing
PrerequisitesQuantum replication
Research cost5000 cycles, 60 seconds each, using all major science packs through promethium science.

Fleet printing unlocks compact fleet bootstrap packs and antimatter propulsion.

Unlocks

  • Ship starter pack
  • Antimatter drive

Strategy

Fleet printing is the capstone expansion technology. Use it after quantum replication can sustain the material and antimatter cost of new fleets and high-speed drives.
