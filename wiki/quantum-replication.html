Quantum replication

Quantum replication
Prototypequantum-replication
PrerequisitesInterstellar fleets
Research cost3000 cycles, 60 seconds each, using all major science packs through promethium science.

Quantum replication turns interstellar dust into the raw and exotic materials needed for self-sufficient interstellar platforms.

Unlocks

  • Quantum replicator
  • Dust fusion energy cell
  • Replicate antimatter
  • Replication recipes for iron ore, copper ore, coal, stone, uranium ore, carbon, ice, scrap, calcite, tungsten ore, holmium ore, lithium, yumako, jellynut, bioflux, biter eggs, pentapod eggs, and promethium asteroid chunks

Strategy

This is the key logistics technology for long-range fleets. It closes gaps that would otherwise require importing planet-specific resources to an interstellar platform.
