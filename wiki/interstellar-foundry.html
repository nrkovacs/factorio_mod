Interstellar foundry

The interstellar foundry is a space-safe foundry variant for platform metallurgy.

Unlocked byOrbital industry
Recipe500 interstellar dust, 100 calcite, 100 quantum processors, 200 low density structures
PlacementSpace platforms and normal surfaces

It keeps the base foundry behavior and animations while removing the original foundry's planet-specific placement restriction. Its ingredients are recoverable through quantum replication, so a mature fleet can build it without importing a planet-made foundry.
