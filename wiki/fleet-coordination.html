Fleet coordination

An infinite research that improves the production speed bonus from consolidated fleet size by 3% per level.

PrerequisiteFleet printing
Cost formula2000 * 1.55 ^ (L - 1)
Effect+3% fleet-size speed bonus per level

Fleet coordination improves the abstract multiplier from merged ships without increasing the fleet-size energy-consumption penalty.
