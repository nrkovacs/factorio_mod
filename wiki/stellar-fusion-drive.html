Stellar fusion drive

Stellar fusion drive
TypeDrive
Prototypestellar-fusion-drive
Unlocked byInterstellar fleets

The Stellar fusion drive is the baseline interstellar propulsion upgrade for relativistic fleet travel.

Obtaining

  • Crafted after researching Interstellar fleets.

Usage

  • Adds one unit of fleet drive power.
  • Consumes fusion power cells during boost operation.

Recipe

AmountIngredient
1Thruster
2Fusion generator
50Quantum processor

Mechanics

Fusion drives turn fusion cells into sustained acceleration. Their fuel cost scales with the number of fusion drives, fleet size, and relativistic gamma so very fast, very large fleets need serious fuel logistics.
