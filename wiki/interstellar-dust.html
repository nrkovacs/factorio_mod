Interstellar dust

Interstellar dust
TypeIntermediate product
Prototypeinterstellar-dust
Unlocked byInterstellar fleets

Interstellar dust is the core off-world feedstock gathered by interstellar fleets and converted into basic materials, fusion fuel, antimatter, and biter eggs by the Quantum replicator.

Obtaining

  • Generated by Interstellar dust collectors while a fleet is on an interstellar route.
  • Production scales with collector count, fleet size, and relativistic speed.

Usage

  • Input for quantum replication recipes.
  • Input for dust fusion energy cells.
  • Input for antimatter replication.
  • Input for replicated biter eggs.

Recipe

This item is generated by scripted fleet mechanics rather than a normal crafting recipe.

Mechanics

Dust represents diffuse matter, exotic isotopes, and recoverable debris gathered outside normal star systems. It is deliberately high volume: the collector produces it continuously, while expensive recipes consume it in bulk.
