Effect

Reduces Antimatter drive antimatter consumption by 10% per completed level, to a minimum of 20% of the base boost cost.

Strategy

This is the premium propulsion optimization chain. It makes antimatter fleets less wasteful over time without making the expensive antimatter economy obsolete.
