Stellar fusion drive efficiency

Research costInfinite research. Cost formula: 1000 ? 1.5^(L - 1), 60 seconds per cycle, using all major science packs through promethium science.

Effect

Reduces Stellar fusion drive fusion power cell consumption by 8% per completed level, to a minimum of 20% of the base boost cost.

Strategy

This is the sustainable long-range travel research for fusion-powered fleets. It rewards repeated promethium-era science investment while preserving a real fuel logistics requirement.
