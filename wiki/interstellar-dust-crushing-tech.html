Interstellar dust crushing

Interstellar dust crushing
Prototypeinterstellar-dust-crushing
PrerequisitesQuantum replication
Research cost2500 cycles, 60 seconds each, using all major science packs through promethium science.

Interstellar dust crushing unlocks an asteroid-crusher recipe that reprocesses interstellar dust into itself while occasionally exposing asteroid chunks.

Unlocks

  • Interstellar dust crushing recipe

InputOutput
100 Interstellar dust60 Interstellar dust
15% chance: Metallic asteroid chunk
15% chance: Carbonic asteroid chunk
15% chance: Oxide asteroid chunk
5% chance: Promethium asteroid chunk

Strategy

This recipe is useful when a fleet has strong dust income and needs normal asteroid materials without relying only on direct replication. It fits Factorio's sorting gameplay: crushers create mixed probabilistic outputs, and the platform must route useful chunks into existing asteroid-processing chains. Productivity is disabled so the recipe cannot become a free dust duplication loop.
